## About Us

**Nousivo LLC** builds AI-powered productivity apps for iOS, Android, and macOS. We believe AI should be powerful, private, and accessible to everyone.

Unlike big tech alternatives, our apps use a **"Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK)** model — you control your AI, and your data never leaves your device.

---

## Our Apps

### 📄 SeekerPDF
**AI PDF Intelligence**

Scan, chat with, and analyze any PDF — privately.

- Camera-to-PDF scanning with OCR
- Chat with documents using natural language
- AI summaries, translations, and analysis
- Works offline with local AI

### 📚 StudyG
**AI Study Companion**

AI-generated flashcards, quizzes, and tutors for smarter studying.

- Instant flashcard generation from any text
- Adaptive quizzes with spaced repetition
- AI tutors for Socratic dialogue and problem-solving
- Study groups with AI buddies

### 💼 MySec
**AI Personal Secretary**

Your AI secretary for writing, career, and productivity.

- AI writing assistant (reports, emails, proposals)
- Resume builder with ATS optimization
- Contract analyzer with red flag detection
- Daily briefings and task management
